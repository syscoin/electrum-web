Warning: Do not download ElectrumSys from another source than electrum.syscoin.org, and learn to verify GPG signatures.

Safe

Your private keys are encrypted and never leave your computer.

Forgiving

Your funds can be recovered from a secret phrase.

Instant On

ElectrumSys is fast, because it uses servers that index the Syscoin blockchain.

No Lock-In

You can export your private keys and use them in other Syscoin clients.

No Downtimes

ElectrumSys servers are decentralized and redundant. Your wallet is never down.

Proof Checking

ElectrumSys Wallet verifies all the transactions in your history using SPV.

Cold Storage

Keep your private keys offline, and go online with a watching-only wallet.

Multisig

Split the permission to spend your coins between several wallets.

Add-ons

ElectrumSys supports third-party plugins: Multisig services, Hardware wallets, etc.
